The "Nutanix Purple" Master Branding

Objective: Design or update visual assets to align with the new Nutanix brand identity. STRICT REQUIREMENT: Transition away from the legacy lime green (#AFD135) and royal blue (#024DA0).

1. Primary Color Palette (The Purple Core):

- Deep Purple (Primary/Background): '#2D1E45' (The "Dark Matter" base for dark-mode slides and headers).
    
- Electric Violet (Primary Accent): '#7A39FB' (Used for primary calls to action, highlights, and active states).
    
- Nutanix White: '#FFFFFF' (Main background for high-readability text-heavy slides).
    
- Slate Gray: '#4A4A4A' (Secondary text and subtle dividers).
    

2. Secondary & Gradient Colors:

- Deep Violet: '#522E91'
    
- Soft Lilac: '#B59BC6' (Used for background textures and secondary iconography).
    
- Vibrant Gradient: Linear gradient from '#2D1E45' (0%) to '#7A39FB' (100%) at a 45-degree angle. Use this for "Cloud Flow" visual motifs.
    

3. Typography & Voice:

- Headline Font: Gotham Rounded (Bold or Medium). If unavailable, use a clean, rounded Sans-Serif like Montserrat or Outfit.
    
- Body Font: Gotham Rounded (Book/Light). Maintain high tracking/letter-spacing for a modern feel.
    

4. Iconography & Graphics:

- The "X" Element: Use the modernized, flat "X" logo. The "V" portion of the X should no longer be lime green; it should be Electric Violet (#7A39FB) or White.
    
- Icon Style: "Technical Line" style. Icons should be thin-stroked, monochromatic (using '#7A39FB' or '#B59BC6'), and devoid of shadows.
    
- Nixi the Yeti: For educational or community content, use the "Nixi" character assets in the "Purple/Soft Gray" colorway (refer to brand.nutanix.com for specific poses).
    

5. Visual Layout Logic:

- The Chevron Border: Use 45-degree angular crops (Chevrons) on image edges or section dividers to mimic the Nutanix logo's "X" geometry.
    
- Negative Space: Keep layouts "Invisible"—minimalist, spacious, and focused on one key message per slide.
    

Reference Material: For all logo lockups and PNG/SVG downloads, refer to brand.nutanix.com.
